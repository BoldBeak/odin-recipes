Fresh Homemade Lemonade


Description

Nothing is better than an ice cold glass of lemonade on a hot day!

Ingredients:

  • A glass or plastic pitcher
  • 4 to six fresh lemons (or about 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar (plus more to taste)
  • 2 quarts cold water
  • ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Add sugar to a 2 quart pitcher.
  2. Juice 4 to 6 fresh lemons until you have about 1/2 cup of juice (or use 1/2 cup of bottled lemon juice)
  3. Add lemon juice to the pitcher.
  4. Fill the pitcher with cold water and stir until sugar is dissolved.
  5. Taste your lemonade at this point and add additional sugar or lemon juice if desired.
  6. Serve in a glass over ice.
