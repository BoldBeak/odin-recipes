Simple Mac & Cheese


Description

This is a simple, stove-top mac and cheese just like grandma used to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter or margerine
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook elbow macaroni per package instructions until it is al dente.
  2. Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat
  3. Stir in flour, salt, and pepper until smooth (about 5 minutes).
  4. Slowly pour milk into butter-flour mixture while stirring continuously until mixture is smooth and bubbling (about 5 minutes).
  5. Add Cheddar Cheese to milk mixture and stir until cheese is melted (about 2 to 4 minutes).
  6. Fold macaroni into cheese sauce until coated.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
